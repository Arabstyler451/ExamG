Our Story

Four generations of farming at Greenacre

Greenacre Farm has been in the Hartley family since 1942. What began as a traditional mixed farm has evolved into a leading organic market garden focused on heirloom varieties, soil health, and minimal environmental impact.

We grow over 60 varieties of vegetables across 45 acres. Every seed is open-pollinated, every harvest recorded, and every batch can be traced from field to plate using our batch codes.

Who We Are

About Greenfield Local Hub

A cooperative of local farmers and food producers working together to bring fresh, traceable food to our community.

Our Mission

Connecting communities through honest food

GLH was founded by a group of local farmers who wanted to cut out the middleman and sell direct to their community. Today we represent 28 farms and food producers, serving over 4,000 customers across the region.

Every product sold through GLH supports a real farmer, keeps money in the local economy, and provides full traceability from farm to fork.

Image
Benefits of buying locally

Benefits of buying locally

Buying locally grown food helps support farmers and producers within your community. It keeps money circulating in the local economy and helps small businesses continue to grow and provide jobs.

Local food is often fresher because it travels a shorter distance from farm to table. This also reduces transportation emissions and allows customers to better understand where their food comes from and how it was produced.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Sustainability

We only partner with producers who demonstrate genuine commitment to sustainable farming practices and environmental stewardship.

Transparency

Every product carries full traceability information so you can see exactly where your food was grown, by whom, and how.

Community

We reinvest in local food networks, support new producers, and run regular community events throughout the year.

Fair Pricing

Producers set their prices freely. GLH takes a small commission on each transaction with complete transparency.

Accessibility

Our platform is designed for everyone. We follow WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and support a wide range of access needs.

Local First

All producers are within 30 miles of our hub. Shorter supply chains mean fresher food, fewer miles, lower emissions.
