Set Task Brief

You work for a software development company. The company has secured a new contract to develop a digital solution for Greenfield Local Hub (GLH). GLH is a cooperative of local farmers and food producers. GLH offers:

a single location retail outlet for locally produced food and drink
collective marketing for local farmers and food producers
product management, stock control and product  traceability.

GLH (the client) would like to develop a digital solution that will:

provide information about the producers, their methods and the benefits of buying locally
allow customers to:

 	- view products with transparent pricing and availability
 	- place orders for collection or delivery
 	- manage their account and view order history

provide a dashboard to enable producers to manage stock levels, update product details and view orders.

The client has conducted market research with existing customers to identify features that could be included in the digital. The features suggested by the client are:

order management, scheduling and tracking features
a loyalty scheme that provides discounts and special offers
accessibility features to support a wide range of users.
